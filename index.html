Series information

"Vikings: Valhalla" is a vibrant historical adventure series from Netflix that continues the epic saga initiated by the original series "Vikings". Set at the dawn of the 11th century, it delves into the legendary tales of iconic figures such as Leif Eriksson, Freydís Eiríksdóttir, and Harald Hardrada, amidst the twilight of the Viking era. Confronting the conflicts between pagan Vikings and the Christian kingdoms of Europe, "Vikings: Valhalla" immerses viewers in spectacular battles, political intrigues, and the unyielding quest for honour and glory. Boasting splendid production values and compelling storytelling, this series promises to captivate fans of the historical and adventure genres, continuing the legacy of its predecessor with new stories of courage and conquest.
